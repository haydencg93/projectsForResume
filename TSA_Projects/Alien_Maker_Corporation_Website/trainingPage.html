-Training-

Traveling to space can be very tough, mentally and physically, so we suggest going through at least one year of training before beginning the appliction process for any of our space traveling programs. We offer intensive launch exposure, vehicle opporation training, and multiple seminars from experienced space explorers.

- Intensive Launch Exposure -

This program is to prepare you for not only the launch and landing, but also the everyday zero-gravity movements you will be making.

- Vehicles -

Generated by Artificial Inteligence (AI)

You will be trained to use any and all vehicles you could find at any of our facilities and in space. Such as gas trucks, high powered golf carts, rockets, and space cars. This also includes our remotely controlled machines such as rovers.

- Seminars and Speakers -

A-M C has recruited space veterans, aerospace technicians, physisists, and current explorers to give our future space explorers more knowledge about every possible aspect of space exploration. Students are required to attend at least 5 seminars.
